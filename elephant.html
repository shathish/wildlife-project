About Tigers

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world. They are known for their beautiful striped coats and are apex predators in their habitats.

About Deer

Deer are graceful herbivores commonly found in forests and grasslands. They are known for their speed and agility.

About Elephants

Elephants are the largest land mammals on Earth. They are highly intelligent and have a strong social structure within their herds.

About This Project

This project was created as a school assignment to explore the beauty of wildlife. Each page highlights a specific animal, providing key facts and images to make learning fun and engaging.
